Peruvian Lomo Saltado

Description

This lomo saltado recipe is a traditional and very easy dish to make. I might add that it's also very popular with finicky kids and adults.

Ingredients

  • Frozen French Fries
  • Beef strips
  • Tomatoes
  • Soy Sauce
  • Rice

Steps

  1. Cook the french fries in a pan or an oven. Peruvians make them in the pan.
  2. Season the beef and cook it.
  3. Add the sliced tomatoes and some soy sauce. Cook until it's tender.
  4. I hope by now that you cooked the rice as well.
  5. Serve a side of rice (cooked) and the beef+tomatoes stir mixed with some french fries. You have to mix the fries with the beef for a true peruvian presentation.
  6. Add some parsley on top, if you have parsley.

You can eat this with an Inca Kola or a cold beer. Maybe even with a Pisco Sour.
