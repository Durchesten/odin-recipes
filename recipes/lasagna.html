Delicious Homemade Lasagna

Description

This is a recipe my mother never bothered to show me, with her famous lasagna. I loved it when I was a kid, and still love it to this day.

She makes this recipe once or twice per year, though. So this adds to the magic of this recipe. When she makes this, you know it's for a special day.

Ingredients

  • Grounded beef
  • Lasagna pasta sheets
  • Some weird white sauce
  • Mozarella Cheese
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Add salt and pepper to the grounded beef.
  2. Cook it in a pan until it's brown and juicy.
  3. On a large recipient that can fit into your oven, add layers of the boiled pasta, the beef and that weird white sauce. Don't forget to put some oil to the walls of the recipient.
  4. Put everything inside the oven and bake until it's ready.

As I told you before, my mother never bothered to teach me her recipe, and I never bothered to try to make it, so I don't guarantee the final results following this recipe.

It's just what I saw she was doing.
