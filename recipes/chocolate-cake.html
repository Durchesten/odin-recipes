Chocolate Cake

Description

After eating a piece of lasagna and an amazing peruvian lomo saltado, what's best than to finish with an amazing, moist chocolate cake?

Yummy!

Ingredients

  • Cocoa powder
  • Flour
  • Baking powder and Baking Soda
  • Eggs
  • Malk. Now with vitamin R
  • Vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven.
  2. Mix all ingredients in a mixer.
  3. Put everything in a baking pan and bake it for about 40 minutes.
